Introduction:

AGE (Another Gameboy Compiler) is a undergraduate project undertaken by its developers as a learning experience in emulation and to gain a deeper understanding of alternative computer architecture. The following documentation was created to act as a publication of our work and a guide for others looking into similar projects.
